TIM DUTCHER

Owner of Spartan Training Center, is A.C.E. (American Council of Exercise) and I.F.P.A. (International Fitness Professionals Association) certified. Tim created the “Spartan Training Center System”. Tim has been training professionally in the Boston area for the last 25 years. He has trained or supervised the training of several hundred clients. Tim is an expert in all facets of exercise science, from weight and strength training to flexibility and cardiovascular conditioning. He brings that level of experience to the Spartan Training Center. The center has operated in the south shore area since 2003 and has been at the forefront of personal training.



DAVID PERRY

David is a graduate of Lasell University with a degree in Exercise Science. He is NASM Certified. David has an extensive athletic background, featuring a captainship of the Men’s Soccer Team at Lasell and is an accomplished tri-state gymnast. David is currently pursuing both jiu-jitsu and muay-thai, further diversifying his athletic portfolio. His passion is being able to change peoples lives and watch people achieve their fitness goals.



ANNIE NGUYEN

Annie is a NASM certified personal trainer. Graduate of Emerson College, she both ran track and played lacrosse. This is where Annie developed her passion for weight lifting and various forms of physical exercise. Currently, she has been practicing Yoga and Meditation to expand her exercise horizons. Annie loves to motivate and encourage her clients to move their bodies, spreading happiness and confidence through physical activity.



TIM DUTCHER JR.
